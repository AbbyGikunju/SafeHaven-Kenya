What is Femicide?

Femicide is the killing of women based on their gender. It represents the extreme violence that women face due to inequality and the patriarchy in Kenyan society. Often, femicide victims are murdered by intimate partners or family members.

Why Address Femicide?

Femicide is not an isolated issue. It’s a systemic problem rooted in deeply embedded social norms. Understanding femicide allows us to address gender-based violence on a broader scale, ensuring that women can live free from fear and violence in every corner of Kenya.
